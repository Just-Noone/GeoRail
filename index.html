About us

GeoRail is a dedicated Minecraft server that was launched in early February 2024, with the vision of creating a thriving community centered around the construction of expansive rail networks and complex structures within a richly detailed world. From the outset, the server attracted a growing community of passionate builders and adventurers eager to leave their mark on the GeoRail universe.

However, the journey was not without its challenges. After months of development and community engagement, the server experienced significant disruptions in late May. A few members joined with malicious intent, causing widespread destruction that severely impacted the server's infrastructure. To make matters worse, the world suffered from corruption, forcing a complete reset of the server.
Faced with these difficulties, the server's owner, JusttNoone, made the tough decision to temporarily shut down GeoRail in mid-June 2024. But this was far from the end. Driven by a commitment to preserving the spirit of the project, JusttNoone took the time to rebuild GeoRail from the ground up, this time with a focus on security, stability, and fostering a close-knit community.
GeoRail has now been relaunched as a private server, open only to trusted members and skilled builders who share a commitment to creativity, collaboration, and respect for the community. The server is stronger than ever, offering a rich and immersive experience where every creation is a testament to the dedication of its members.
Welcome to GeoRail, where your imagination and craftsmanship can thrive in a world built on trust and shared passion.
